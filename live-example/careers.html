Are you looking for a rewarding career in special education? NECC is a global leader is autism education and research, serving students through residential and day programs in Southborough, MA, in public school classrooms across New England, and in clinics in the Middle East.

Join our team and help children with autism learn and grow.

Students between the ages of 3 and 22 receive year-round services with science-backed curriculum ranging from basic prerequisite skills for learning, including attention, imitation and self-care, to academic skills, such as reading, math and science.
