Boston Marathon

Since 2007, Team NECC runners have raised nearly $1 million through the BAA’s Boston Marathon charity program. Those who would like to run for Team NECC are required to raise a minimum of $10,000 and must pay the $375 B.A.A. entry fee. NECC provides the following for all accepted runners:

Benefits
  • Charity bib fee
  • Professional coaching through the Marathon Coalition
  • Personal fundraising webpage (through GivenGain)
  • Comprehensive fundraising support
  • Team NECC singlet
  • Runner gift/swag
Application

We are currently in the process of applying for the Bank of America Boston Marathon Charity Program to have the opportunity to bring runners to the 129th Boston Marathon presented by Bank of America on April 21, 2025. Please check back in the fall for more information.

Falmouth Road Race

As part of the Numbers for Nonprofits Program, NECC receives bibs for this scenic 7-mile race each year. Those who run for Team NECC’s Falmouth Road Race team commit to raising a minimum of $1,200. NECC provides the following for all accepted runners:

Benefits
  • Race entry
  • Personal fundraising webpage (through Race Roster)
  • Comprehensive fundraising support
  • Team NECC singlet
  • Runner gift/swag
Application

Thank you to our 2024 Falmouth Road Race team, who raised nearly $38,000 for NECC! The 53rd ASICS Falmouth Road Race is schedule for August 17, 2025. Please check back for in early 2025 for instructions on how to represent Team NECC in this incredible race!
