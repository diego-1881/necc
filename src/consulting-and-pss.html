Specialized Consulting
and Classroom Services

Our mission is to teach students with autism the skills necessary to function effectively within their school, community, and families by providing exceptional instruction within their home district.

Through our Consulting and Public School Services Program, The New England Center for Children® (NECC®) collaborates with over 100 public schools across New England through model classrooms and consultation.

Partner Program Classrooms

Building Skills for School and Community Success

Our comprehensive Partner Program Classrooms includes a full-time master’s-level certified lead teacher, weekly clinical supervision from a NECC BCBA®, ACE® ABA Software System curriculum, as well as modification of general education curriculum, and comprehensive staff training and ongoing mentorship.

Partner Program Classrooms are a cost-effective way for school districts to improve their services for special education students.

Cooperative Classrooms

Partnership for Effective Learning and Support

Our Cooperative Classrooms work in a similar manner but with a district-hired full-time certified teacher, direct-care paraprofessionals, and weekly administrative support to the teacher and classroom.

NECC provides weekly consultation from a senior-level BCBA, access to the ACE® ABA Software System, and staff training.

Consultation

NECC consultants provide short- and long-term consultation to school districts to provide expertise in behavior reduction and skill acquisition programming, while building the capacity of district staff to effectively serve students with autism.

Evaluations

Experienced NECC consultants provide in-depth and objective evaluations of students, programs, and districts, identifying areas of strength and areas for improvement, along with recommendations to achieve goals.

Staff Training

NECC offers professional development sessions on topics in special education and supporting students with autism and related needs. Training sessions can be scheduled on-site at your school or agency or conducted virtually. Examples of training include:

  • Live ABA trainings and virtual webinars for BCBAs
  • Customized training content for a range of audiences and/or developed in specific content areas
  • CALM Crisis Intervention Training
  • ABAplus® Online Learning

For more information or to schedule a training, please contact Erin Coulson.
