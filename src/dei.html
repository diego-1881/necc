Supporting Employee Resource Groups (ERGs)

Providing safe spaces for staff to connect, learn, and grow through our seven ERGs.

Employee Resource Groups

NECC has seven ERGs designed to offer dedicated spaces of support, community, and learning for our staff:

  • Black & Brown & All Colors
  • Inclusivity Across Borders
  • Neurodiversity
  • Pride Alliance
  • Residential Counselors
  • Wellness
  • Working Caregivers

These ERGs are an essential component of our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion. They help foster a sense of belonging and promote learning and understanding throughout our organization.
