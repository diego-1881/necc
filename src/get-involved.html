Parents

NECC values parent/caregiver involvement in our school community. From the Parent/Caregiver Organization to volunteer opportunities at student events such as Field Day and holiday dances, there are many ways in which parents and caregivers can get involved at NECC.

Events

Throughout the year, NECC hosts events to raise money for the Annual Fund.

The two annual flagship events are the Children of Promise Gala (November) and the NECC 5K Walk/Run for Autism (May).

If you are interested in volunteering or sponsoring an event, visit the Events page for more information.
